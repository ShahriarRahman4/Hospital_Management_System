New Variants Detected & Rising Cases

Two new Omicron subvariants, XFG and XFC, have been detected in Bangladesh. According to icddr,b surveillance data from nine districts—including Rajshahi, Cumilla, Khulna, Jashore, Sylhet, Barishal, Chattogram, Dinajpur, and Kishoreganj—COVID-19 cases have increased noticeably. In May 2025, around 7% of patients at study hospitals tested positive for the new variants.

Health Guidelines

Essential prevention measures and symptoms to watch for

Prevention Measures
  • Wear masks in public places and crowded areas
  • Maintain physical distance of at least 6 feet
  • Wash hands frequently with soap and water for 20 seconds
  • Use hand sanitizer with at least 60% alcohol
  • Avoid touching face, especially eyes, nose, and mouth
  • Cover coughs and sneezes with tissue or elbow
  • Stay home if you feel sick and seek medical attention
  • Get vaccinated and follow booster dose schedule
Symptoms to Watch For
Common Symptoms:
  • Fever or chills
  • Dry cough
  • Fatigue
  • Body aches
  • Loss of taste or smell
  • Sore throat
Severe Symptoms (Seek immediate care):
  • Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
  • Persistent chest pain or pressure
  • Confusion or inability to stay awake
  • Bluish lips or face
